About the Artist

Meet Noel

Noel is a gifted young artist whose paintings are full of colour, feeling, and a perspective on the world that is entirely his own. He discovered a love for painting early in life and has never looked back.

Noel is on the autism spectrum, and his art reflects a remarkable ability to focus deeply, notice details others miss, and translate those observations into vivid, layered canvases. His process is patient and deliberate — each painting is built up slowly, sometimes over weeks.

"Painting is how I tell people what I see."

His work has been shown in local art galleries and continues to attract admirers who connect with the quiet honesty of his paintings. When he is not painting, Noel enjoys spending time outdoors, listening to music, and visiting galleries to look at work by other artists he admires.
